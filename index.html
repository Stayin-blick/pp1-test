Kami

Kami is a unique generative collection of rebellious, non-conforming gods-in-training searching for their true calling.


In the realm of the divine, amidst the creation of Kamis by the elder gods, there arose two exceptional and distinctive groups that stood apart from the multitude. These were the Sarus and the Kindreds, a rare breed among the 4269 Kamis, renowned for their mischievous nature and insatiable thirst for attention. With only 364 members combined, they delighted in playing pranks on their fellow Kamis, always seeking to capture the spotlight.
